Business

Businesses form the backbone of the consortium. Their role is to provide the industry knowledge and expertise on the cybersecurity needs of the region, and identify the workforce skills necessary for the field.

OBJECTIVES

As part of this role, the workforce agencies will:

  • Promote the curriculum & training programs within the business community.

  • Refer incumbent workers for skills upgrade and certification acquisition.

  • Assist in identifying subject matter experts.

  • Provide internships or other learning opportunities for individuals interested in cybersecurity careers.

  • Hire successful completers of cybersecurity training.
